Recognizing Problem Gambling:

It's important to be aware of the signs of problem gambling, for yourself or for someone you know. Consider these questions:

  • Do you spend more money and time gambling than you can afford?
  • Do you gamble to escape from problems or feelings of sadness?
  • Do you try to win back losses with more gambling?
  • Do you feel guilty or anxious about your gambling?
  • Have you lied to hide the extent of your gambling?
  • Has gambling negatively impacted your relationships, work, or finances?

If you answered yes to several of these questions, you might be experiencing a gambling problem.
